About

Algorithms should be measurable, inspectable, and useful in the loop.

I work on search, query understanding, knowledge graphs, entity linking, question answering, and agentic AI systems. My sweet spot is turning a promising model behavior into a system that can be evaluated, debugged, shipped, and improved.

This site is structured as an AI-focused portfolio: selected algorithmic projects, technical interests, and notes on building trustworthy learning systems.
